Welcome!

Breezemont, founded in 1936, is a co-ed day camp for children ages 3-13, located on 18 beautiful acres in Armonk, NY. We specialize in creating valuable summer experiences that our campers remember for a lifetime. Summer after summer, families return to Breezemont for its encouraging atmosphere, wide array of activities, and thriving sense of community. Project Description

This website is a school project by Estee Bruno and Danielle Sobel. We used a drone to record and take pictures of the Breezemont Day Camp during Fall 2016. For this project, we created a website that shows an interactive map where parents and children can use it. The map shows the entire camp and describes what each area is and the history. The vintage home that is located in the front of the camp, was made into a 3D render. Since the camp will be doing contruction, we wanted to preserve the original athestics digitally so that everyone can see it.
