Welcome!

Breezemont, founded in 1936, is a co-ed day camp for children ages 3-13, located on 18 beautiful acres in Armonk, NY.
We specialize in creating valuable summer experiences that our campers remember for a lifetime. Summer after summer, families return to Breezemont for its encouraging atmosphere, wide array of activities, and thriving sense of community.

Project Description

This website is a school project from The New School by Estee Bruno and Danielle Sobel. We used a drone to record and take pictures of the Breezemont Day Camp during Fall 2016. For this project, we created a website that shows an interactive map where parents and children can use it. The map shows the entire camp and describes what each area is called. The vintage home that is located in the front of the camp, was made into a 3D render. Since the camp will be doing construction, we wanted to preserve the original athestics digitally so that everyone can see it. Danielle added another component from her Advanced Javascript by making an app to make drawings and colors of the camp.

History

The main house was built in the 1860s. It was the main focus of a summer resort of New York City families. It served as a hotel and boarding house from the early 1900's. In 1936, it finally became an offical camp in Weschester County.
